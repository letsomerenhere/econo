Our Story

Econo Service Agency, Inc. was founded in 2012 with the vision of serving the Korean-American community in Georgia and beyond. Our goal has always been to provide honest, accessible, and multilingual support for insurance, translation, and real estate needs under one roof.

Meet the Owner

Peter Kong, founder and licensed agent, brings over two decades of experience in finance, insurance, and cross-cultural consulting. Born in South Korea and now based in Suwanee, GA, Peter’s bilingual expertise and commitment to service have guided thousands of clients through important personal and business decisions.

A Letter from Peter

Dear clients and friends,

Welcome to Econo Service Agency, Inc.—your trusted partner in insurance, real estate, and language support. I founded this company not only as a licensed insurance agent and real estate broker, but also as a member of the Korean-American community who understood the real barriers people face when trying to navigate important life systems in a different language.

At Econo Service Agency, we combine years of experience and cultural understanding to offer dependable support in insurance, property transactions, and interpretation. We built this company for people like you—families seeking financial security, individuals buying their first home, and immigrants who need more than just translation; they need someone who understands.

Whether you’re a Korean speaker looking for clear communication, or someone seeking bilingual professionals who take the time to truly listen, we are here to guide you with patience and honesty.

What makes us different is not only what we offer—but why. This business started out of love for our community and a desire to see people protected, understood, and empowered. Our team is fluent in both Korean and English, with a client-first mindset and deep respect for every situation you bring to us.

Thank you for visiting our website. We’re here for you whenever you need guidance, answers, or peace of mind.

With gratitude,

Peter Kong
Founder & CEO, Econo Service Agency, Inc.
